Deciding where to give away your money can be a daunting choice. In comes Charity Navigator, an organization dedicated to giving an unbiased, objective, numbers-based" rating system to help you give intelligently. While designing this project, we found an excellent, fairly clean data set that was over 8,000 records of unique charitable organization data. This site brings key aspects of that data and Charity Navigator's mission to life. Please feel free to explore our visuals and see where your next giving dollars will go! [As a short disclaimer: we are in no way affiliated or connected with Charity Navigator.]
